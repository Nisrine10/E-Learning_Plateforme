About Us

Opened in 1979, the French International School of Oregon (formerly the French American International School) cultivates intellect and character through rigorous multilingual academic programs in an environment that promotes appreciation of diverse cultures and experiences. Discover why families believe their child’s future begins at the French International School of Oregon: the longest-established language immersion school in Portland—and the only school in Portland authorized by the International Baccalaureate (IB) for preschool-8th grade.
